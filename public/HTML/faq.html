FAQs

Q. What is the JEE Main 2022 revised date of April session exam? +

Ans: The JEE Main new dates 2022 are April 21, 24, 25, 29 and May 1, 4, 2022.

Q: I graduated in 2021 but I do not have my degree certificate or provisional certificate. What document should I upload to prove that I am eligible for GATE 2022? +

Ans : You can upload a copy of your final year mark sheet OR a declaration from your college Principal (or other competent authority) along with the signature and seal

Q. How many times is NEET held in a year +

Ans : The NTA conducts the NEET exam once every year.

Q. Are there any limits to attempting the NEET exam? +

Ans : There is an age limit of 17 years. You have to be 17 or more till the time of admission. If you do not fulfil this criterion, you cannot take admissions to any medical college. Other than this, there are no limitations.

Q. Who is the authorising body to conduct NEET? +

Ans : National Testing Agency (NTA) is the conducting body of the NEET exam.

Q: I am a Postgraduate. Can I appear for GATE 2022? What are the documents I need to upload while applying? +

Ans: In your case, you will have to upload the documents related to the relevant qualifying degree while filling the application form.

Q. What is unique about the NEET exam? +

Ans: Earlier, Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER) and the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) conducted separate exams for medical admissions. But, now NEET is in charge of conducting the medical entrance exam. You have to clear the NEET exam to take admission into any prestigious medical college in India. Candidates qualifying NEET exam will be eligible to get admissions into 76928 Medical, 26949 Dental, 52720 AYUSH, 1205 AIIMS and 200 JIPMER seats.

Q: I belong to OBC category and therefore want to upload my category certificate but I can't find any option to upload the certificate. What to do? +

Ans : It is because category certificate for the OBC candidates is not needed while applying for GATE 2022. The category certificate is used for the purpose of ascertaining the concession offered in the application fee. The OBC certificate would be needed while taking admission using GATE scores.

Q: I have completed my graduation through distance learning. Do you think I can apply for GATE 2022? +

Ans : Yes, you can very much apply for GATE 2022 exam. Please make sure that your course on the basis of which you are applying must be recognised by AICTE.

Q: Please tell me how many times can I appear for GATE? Is there any age limit? +

Ans : You can appear for GATE as many times as you want and yes, there is no age limit too.
