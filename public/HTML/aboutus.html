About US

Master-U has been established as a premier, specialist, autonomous and self-sustained testing organization to conduct mock test for your examination admissions/fellowship in higher educational institutions

Our Mission

To improve equity and quality in education by administering research based valid, reliable, efficient, transparent, fair and international level assessments. The best subject matter experts, psychometricians and IT delivery and security professionals will ensure that the current gaps in existing assessment systems are properly identified and bridged.

core values

Master-U will create a system which will promote books, videos and past year question paper for JEE, NEET, GATE , learning (by students) and assessment .NTA strongly believes in quality, efficiency, effectiveness, equity and security of assessments.

OBJECTIVES

To conduct efficient, transparent college standards mock tests in order to assess the competency of candidates for Exams, and recruitment purposes.
To undertake research on educational, professional and testing systems to identify gaps in the knowledge systems and take steps for bridging them.
To identify experts and whether they are ready to write Exams for institutions in setting examination questions.
To produce and disseminate information and research on education and professional development standards.
